However, my current research interest is <b>blockchain-based trust and reputation management for e-commerce platforms</b>, with a focus on transparent <b>trust scoring, fake review detection, privacy-aware digital transactions, and AI-assisted review integrity</b>.

I am seeking <em>HDR/MRes/MPhil</em> supervision and <em>RTP scholarship</em> opportunities for 2027 in IT/Computer Science, particularly in blockchain, trust management, cybersecurity, e-commerce reputation systems, applied AI, and information systems.

<p style="text-align: justify">

  <b>Proposed Research Direction</b>

A Blockchain-Based Trust Management Framework with AI-Assisted Fake Review Detection for E-Commerce Platforms

This proposed research aims to explore how blockchain can support transparent, tamper-resistant, and privacy-aware trust management in e-commerce reputation systems. The project may also investigate AI-assisted methods for detecting fake or unreliable reviews and improving the reliability of trust scores in online marketplaces.
